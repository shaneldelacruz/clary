About Us

The mission behind Clary is to aid students who want to be well informed about classes and teachers at SJSU. As the new school year starts students question if they will be able to succeed in classes without knowing the curriculum or the teacher. That transition between highschool and college is complicated for some students since in college you must pick your own classes which many seem overwhelming to some students who do not know any teachers or teacher styles on campus. How could a student be able to know what they are choosing, if they don't now how the teacher is? That's where clary comes in. Clary is a website that allows students to compare the different lecture styles of different professors. Throughout the website the students find the overview of what the class appear to look like with certain professors.

How do I use Clary? Clary gives certain information depending on what you search for you will be able to find lecture videos, class comments, teacher time schedule etc...From our home page you will see a search tab at the top center. By clicking the button you find a page with the list professors which you can then click on to lead you to a new page which information about the professor.
